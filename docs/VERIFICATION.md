# Verification Workflow

OpenReason frameworks should be checked against the sources they cite.

## Verification levels

- `draft`: initially written, not checked.
- `reviewed`: checked by a contributor against secondary knowledge.
- `verified`: checked against the cited original source.
- `contested`: known disagreement or uncertainty.

## What to verify

For each framework:

1. Are the core concepts real concepts in the source tradition?
2. Are the analysis steps faithful to the source?
3. Are decision rules too strong or too speculative?
4. Are limitations clearly stated?
5. Are references specific enough to review?

## Important caution

Do not treat a framework as scientifically verified just because it appears in this repository. The repository tracks verification status; it does not magically create it.
