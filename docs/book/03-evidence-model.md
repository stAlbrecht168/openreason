# Evidence Model

The evidence model is the core of OpenReason.

It prevents a common mistake: jumping from a quote directly to a claim about hidden intention.

Example:

```text
[O2] The speaker contrasts Iranians and Somalis.
→ [L1] Acceptance of one group does not logically justify rejection of another.
→ [D1] Iranians may function as a contrast group.
→ [S1] This may reinforce a hierarchy of acceptable minorities.
→ [H1] It may provide plausible deniability, but intent is not proven.
```
